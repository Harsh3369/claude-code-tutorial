Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account details — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information. Spendly is free to use and processes no financial transactions on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Service:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and breakdowns.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app performance.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — loads typography assets. Google's privacy policy applies to these requests.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the data we hold about you, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe.
